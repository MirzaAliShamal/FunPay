Age of Wonders 4 is a strategy game in a magical setting from Paradox Interactive. Exciting tactical turn-based battles and management of your own magical kingdom awaits you. When you finally find yourself in the world, you will begin to develop the capital and surrounding areas, this part of the gameplay is very similar to the Sid Meier’s Civilization series of games. Capture neighboring cells, build mines and sawmills, defend against monsters and improve relationships with your neighbors. Age of Wonders 4 has four ways to achieve victory: expansion, winning on points, destroying all enemy lords, and magic, for which you need to study the elements, build three altars and hold them for 15 turns. In addition to you, there will be city-states on the map; with the right policies, they can later be annexed or used as a base for the production of armies. Sometimes you will come across places fenced with a red line - these are the lairs of monsters; if they are not destroyed, the monsters will multiply and cause a lot of problems. There are also neighboring powers, you will be friends with them if the worldviews converge and no one claims each other’s territory, or you will fight and absorb them if interests intersect.
