It's rare to find hardcore projects among mobile games, but sometimes games are released for non-casuals, one such game being Arena Breakout. This is a looter-shooter where a player, in pursuit of treasures, can lose all their equipment and inventory. Alone or as part of a team, depending on the chosen mode, you will be transported to a random point on the map and will fight other players. If you get killed, everything your character had on them and in their bags will be lost, and you will be transported back to the shelter empty-handed. Matches last approximately half an hour, and during this time you need to stay highly focused because an enemy can be anywhere: on a roof, behind a door, or right behind you. You need to constantly listen for the sound of gunfire or the groans of the dying. There is no mini-map in the game, and enemy figures are not highlighted, so if you are a beginner, you will die often until you get the hang of it. It's better to get used to the game when enemies are not killing you with a single hit, but at the same time, you will need a set of equipment that you are not afraid to lose. We offer to buy Arena Breakout items, and experienced players will select a good yet inexpensive set of gear for you. With medkits and armor-piercing ammunition, you will easily deal with bots and play on equal terms with real players.

As you level up and get better at the game, new maps with stronger enemies and valuable loot will become available to you. These maps feature special locations where top-tier weapons and armor spawn more frequently, but the entrances are usually blocked by locked doors that require keys to open. Additionally, such places attract other well-armed players, resulting in frequent ambushes and shootouts. If you want to try your luck, you'll definitely need top-tier armor and weapons, as well as a supply of keys, medkits, and ammunition. If your last expedition didn’t go well and you ended up with nothing, the best option for you would be to buy Arena Breakout items, gear up, and return to Kamona. Kill enemies, loot their gear, and return to the shelter with a mountain of cash and weapons.
