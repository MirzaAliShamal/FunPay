Arena Breakout can rightfully be called an Escape from Tarkov for mobile devices. This is a realistic first-person shooter with hardcore gameplay where, upon defeat, you literally lose everything. You and several other players are dropped into various locations on the map, and you must reach evacuation points to escape. Along the way, you need to enter abandoned buildings and loot money, ammunition, weapons, and armor. However, there's another strategy — you can hide in the bushes or on rooftops, waiting for players loaded with loot to make their way to the exit. It's best to do this with good weapons and powerful ammo, otherwise you risk becoming prey yourself.

Before heading to a location, you can purchase equipment from the market or agents, but first, you need to buy Arena Breakout Koens because nothing is given away for free. The market isn't available right away, but agents are ready to trade even with newcomers. Joel Garrison has a wide selection of pistols and ammunition, Evita handles medical supplies and will share them with you for a corresponding fee, and Dike Vinson can provide bulletproof vests. Good gear will help you survive a couple of stray bullets, and powerful weapons can take down an enemy with a single shot, especially if you aim for the head.

The more often you return from raids alive, the faster you'll level up and unlock the market, other agents, and, importantly, additional maps. A new player only has access to the "Farm Lands," while other locations with more valuable loot will initially be closed. But once you level up a bit, you’ll be able to access them, but don’t forget to stock up on keys first. These, along with many other items, can be found on the market, but we offer you to buy Arena Breakout Koens first so you have something to pay for the goods. Keys are needed to open locked doors and crates, behind which you'll find piles of money and top-tier weapons. Stuff your backpacks, run to the flare, and evacuate to the safe house to rest and then return to the streets of Kamona.
