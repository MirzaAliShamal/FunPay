Armored Core VI: Fires of Rubicon is a game from the creators of the Dark Souls and Elden Ring series. You will find an action game about combat mechs with vertical gameplay and epic battles. The future turned out to be not as joyful as it seemed, huge corporations captured the planet and are fighting for spheres of influence, and you are just a simple mercenary who is trying to earn money by fulfilling their orders. You do not have to play the previous five parts to understand what is happening here, just take the next task and go to clean up the location. There are different types of missions, both quick ones: go in, kill a few hostile robots, get a reward, and adventures for an hour or more. If you want to try to stop a giant walking carrier, then you should definitely buy Armored Core VI: Fires of Rubicon account and go on a mission. You'll be chasing cargo ships in the sky and destroying them, running away from an explosion through mine tunnels, and fighting the autonomous UPI mech Baltheus, which is as challenging as Midir from Dark Souls 3.

The bosses in the game are different, with their own skills, phases and attack patterns, for almost each you need to select a special tactic and change equipment to fight more effectively. Some of the hostile mechs will have to be destroyed at close range, using a blade, while others require powerful turrets to knock down their overload bar and put them into a state of shock for a couple of seconds. The game has a hangar where you can rearrange the limbs and re-arm the robot, making it lighter and more maneuverable or, conversely, adding weight and combat power. Strong guns are heavy and require more energy, which means you need improved mounts and a powerful generator. The arsenal is also huge, you can choose from more than 40 types of weapons: machine guns, pistols, sniper rifles, bomb throwers, rockets and much more. Only buying Armored Core VI: Fires of Rubicon account separates you from creating a flying tracked tank, painting it pink and blowing up all the enemies.
